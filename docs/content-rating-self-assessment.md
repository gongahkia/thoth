## Current Content Scope

Thoth is an institutional-horror tactical RPG with fantasy combat, stress, death, injury, disease, body-horror text, and morally compromised extraction.

Current content rules set these ceilings:

- Body horror stays brief, readable, and mechanical.
- Violence stays tense and physical, not splatter-first.
- No explicit sexual violence.
- No eroticized violence, torture spectacle, lingering mutilation, or shock-only body detail.
- UI copy stays operational even when narration is grim.

Known content categories:

- Violence: turn-based attacks, enemy skills, death-door/death outcomes, boss encounters, and grim combat narration.
- Blood/body horror: textual references to blood, injuries, preserved bodies, salt, glass, ash, pressure, disease, suturing, draining, and bodies used as records.
- Fear/horror: sustained oppressive tone, disturbing institutional procedures, hostile architecture, survivor exploitation, and psychological stress.
- Language: no verified strong-language system or prose pass.
- Sex/nudity: none in current scope.
- Gambling: no casino-style gambling, paid random items, or real-money wagering in current scope.
- Drugs: disease/medicine systems exist; no illegal drug-use depiction in current scope.
- Online/interactivity: no online interaction, user-generated content, location sharing, or in-game purchases in current scope.

## Preliminary Rating Targets

ESRB:

- [Inference] Target: Mature 17+.
- Rationale: ESRB Mature may include intense violence, blood and gore, sexual content, or strong language. Thoth currently avoids sexual content and strong language, but the body-horror premise and repeated injury/death themes make Mature the safer target than Teen.
- Likely descriptors to prepare for: Violence or Fantasy Violence, Blood, Blood and Gore if mutilation/body-part text or visuals remain prominent.

PEGI:

- [Inference] Target: PEGI 16.
- Rationale: PEGI describes 16/18 violence as increasingly realistic-looking and uses Horror for higher-rated games with moderate or intense sustained horror sequences or disturbing images. Thoth's tone is sustained horror with tactical violence, but current rules avoid graphic spectacle.
- Escalate to PEGI 18 if production adds graphic gore toward human characters, torture spectacle, explicit sexual violence, or gambling mechanics.
- Likely descriptors to prepare for: Violence, Horror.

IARC:

- Use the IARC questionnaire on participating storefronts. IARC assigns age ratings, content descriptors, and interactive elements by region after questionnaire completion.
- [Inference] Expect region-specific mappings close to ESRB Mature / PEGI 16 if the current content ceiling holds.

## Store Page Warning Draft

Content warning: fantasy violence, body horror, injury and disease themes, death, psychological stress, institutional abuse, survivor exploitation, and sustained horror atmosphere. No explicit sexual content or sexual violence.

## Reassessment Triggers

Re-run this assessment before release if any of these ship:

- Graphic gore art, mutilation cutscenes, or lingering anatomy detail.
- Strong profanity.
- Sexual content, nudity, or any sexual violence.
- Drug-use depiction beyond medicine/disease treatment.
- Simulated gambling, paid random items, or real-money purchases.
- Online chat, user-generated content, or user location sharing.
