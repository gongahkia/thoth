# Thoth Product Notes

Date: 2026-06-19

Thoth is now positioned as a compact Lua/LOVE isometric expedition RPG.

## Reboot Position

- Keep deterministic simulation and CLI-verifiable tests.
- Retain the isometric world renderer for exploration.
- Make the active loop about party attrition, stress, light, provisions, rank combat, and estate recovery.
- Keep lore, naming, UI, and mechanics original rather than copying any one reference game.

## Near-Term Product Risk

- The current slice is intentionally small.
- The first quality test is whether movement, scouting, light loss, curios, camping, and a short combat encounter are legible in LOVE.
- Additional content should come after combat controls and state feedback are easy to read.
