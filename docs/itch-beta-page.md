## Description

Thoth is a turn-based expedition RPG built in LOVE2D. Lead ruined specialists through hostile institutional spaces, manage torchlight and provisions, take contracts from survivor enclaves, and decide whether the Estate seals, repairs, extracts, or quietly fails.

This beta contains the Phase 5 content-scale build:

- 35 registry missions across Buried Archive, Salt Cistern, and Ember Warrens.
- Eight classes: Warden, Duelist, Apothecary, Arcanist, Thief, Chirurgeon, Exile, Lamplighter.
- Four ending routes: Estate Seal, Repair Compact, Extraction Collapse, Quiet Failure.
- Boss routes for Codex Reeve, Bell Diver, and Kiln Prioress.
- Estate prep, recruitment, recovery, provisions, trinkets, camping, combat, save/load, keyboard/controller input.
- Accessibility toggles for colorblind modes, high contrast, subtitles, font scale, and reduced motion.

## Controls

- Move: `WASD`
- Interact: `Space`
- Pause: `Escape`
- Confirm/menu: `Enter`
- Settings: available from title and pause.
