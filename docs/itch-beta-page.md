## Description

Thoth is a compact XCOM-lite tactics game built in LOVE/Lua. Lead six auditors through the Buried Archive, read enemy intent, move through fog, use cover and flanks, declare overwatch, and solve objective pressure through deterministic AP choices.

This beta page should describe the tactical beta candidate, not the retired broad-content RPG plan:

- One vertical-slice route: Buried Archive.
- Six-class starting squad: Warden, Duelist, Apothecary, Thief, Arcanist, Lamplighter.
- Six ordered procedural mission variants with fixed-seed validation.
- Tactical systems: AP, cover edges, LoS, flanking, overwatch cones, fog-of-war, hidden/revealed intent, tile inspector, and intent legend.
- Objective pressure: entry audit, shelf protection, proof extraction, ledger repair, sealed shortcut, and Vault Regent final.
- Selected route threats: Shelf Knight elite pressure and Vault Regent boss procedure.
- Save/load, deterministic replay proof, keyboard/controller input, and accessibility settings.

Salt Cistern, Ember Warrens, wider class unlocks, and long-form campaign systems are future content until they pass the same tactical readability and validator gates.

## Controls

- Cursor / aim tile: `WASD`, D-pad, or left stick.
- Select / commit: mouse left click, `Enter`, or controller `A`.
- Inspect preview: `Space` or controller `X`.
- Rotate view: `[` / `]` or controller shoulders.
- End turn: `E`.
- Pause/back: `Escape` or controller back.
- Settings: available from title and pause.
