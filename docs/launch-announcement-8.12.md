## r/IndieDev

```text
Title: Thoth is out now - turn-based expedition RPG about debt, light, and extraction

Thoth is a turn-based expedition RPG where every dungeon run is also an accounting problem.

You lead a roster through hostile institutional spaces, manage torchlight, provisions, stress, and debt, then decide when to extract before the route prices the party out of returning.

Features:
- Rank-based tactical combat
- Stress, afflictions, virtues, death's door, retreat pressure
- Three zones: Buried Archive, Salt Cistern, Ember Warrens
- Estate management, class unlocks, town events, faction pressure
- Save/load, deterministic replays, keyboard/controller support
```
