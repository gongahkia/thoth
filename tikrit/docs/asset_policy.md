# Asset Policy

Tikrit must not copy Microcraft sprites, sounds, icons, screenshots, palettes, tilesheets, or packaged resources.

Allowed asset sources:

- original project-local art
- newly created art made specifically for Tikrit
- CC0 or otherwise compatible third-party art with attribution
- generated bitmap assets when their prompts and outputs are distinct from Microcraft assets

Rules:

- Microcraft source code may be read as a systems reference only.
- Do not import `res/tiles.png`, sounds, logos, icons, screenshots, or derived crops from Microcraft.
- Do not recreate Microcraft sprites by tracing or close visual matching.
- New assets must be listed in `docs/asset_sources.md` with source, license, and usage.
- Placeholder vector/fallback drawing is acceptable while dedicated Tikrit assets are missing.
